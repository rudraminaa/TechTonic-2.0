Why TechTonic-2.0?


A platform designed to help beginners grow with confidence.

Beginner Friendly

TechTonic provides structured tasks that help first-year students learn web development step by step without feeling overwhelmed.

Open Source Learning

Contributors get real open-source experience by working with GitHub workflows, pull requests, and collaborative development.

Mentor Guided

Mentors review submissions and guide contributors, ensuring learning is prioritized over just completing tasks.
